Breakfast Sammie

Description:

It's the perfect breakfast sandwhich for on the go.

Ingrediants:

  • English Muffin
  • One Egg
  • Sliced Deli Turkey
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps:

  1. Toast English Muffin
  2. Cook Egg
  3. Add turkey and cheese to english muffin
  4. Microwave for 30 seconds
  5. Finish assembly
  6. Enjoy!
