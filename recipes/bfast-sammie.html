Breakfast Sammie

Description:

It's the perfect breakfast sandwhich for on the go.

Ingrediants:

  • English Muffin
  • One Egg
  • Sliced Deli Turkey
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps:

  1. Toast English Muffin
  2. Cook Egg
  3. Add turkey and cheese to english muffin
  4. Microwave for 30 seconds
  5. Finish assembly
  6. Enjoy!

Lunch Wrap

Description:

A great healthy and quick luch idea.

Ingrediants:

  • Tortillia
  • Deli Turkey
  • Lettuce
  • Avocado
  • Mayo
  • Tomato

Steps:

  1. Place tortillia on plate
  2. Spread mayo on tortillia
  3. Add lettuce, turkey, avocado, and tomato
  4. Fold wrap like burrito with one end left open

Pasta

Description:

Easiest dinner in the world with minimal steps and clean up.

Ingrediants:

  • Bow-ties or any other form of pasta
  • Marinarra sauce
  • Ground beef or turkey
  • Parmesean cheese

Steps:

  1. Add pasta to bowl with water and bring to bowl
  2. Add ground meat to pan and let cook (season to preference)
  3. When everything is done cooking, add all of it to a bowl or plate
  4. Top with parmesean cheese
